Government Health Programs and Initiatives

The Government of India has launched several initiatives to ensure the health and well-being of its citizens. These programs aim to provide affordable healthcare, improve public health infrastructure, promote mental wellness, and create awareness about preventive healthcare practices.

Major Health Schemes & Programs

Ayushman Bharat - Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY)

One of the world’s largest health assurance schemes providing free medical treatment up to ₹5 lakh per family per year for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization. It focuses on the poor and vulnerable sections of society.

Fit India Movement

A nationwide initiative to encourage people to include physical activity and sports in their daily lives. It aims to promote a culture of fitness and well-being among all citizens, especially youth.

Swachh Bharat Abhiyan

A cleanliness mission launched to improve sanitation, eliminate open defecation, and promote hygiene practices. Clean surroundings directly contribute to better health and reduced disease transmission.

National Health Mission (NHM)

A major umbrella program integrating various national health initiatives to strengthen the healthcare system, reduce infant and maternal mortality, and ensure equitable access to quality health services.

Poshan Abhiyaan

Also known as the National Nutrition Mission, it aims to reduce malnutrition among children, pregnant women, and lactating mothers through nutritional education, awareness, and targeted interventions.

Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan (PMSMA)

This scheme provides free health check-ups for pregnant women on the 9th of every month, ensuring safe pregnancies and reducing maternal and infant mortality rates.
